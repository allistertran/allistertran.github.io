A B O U T

With ‘prompte’ you can have your own personal lifestyle and weather application that enhances your outfits, health, beauty and activity during the day. This is your one stop go for all of your health, beauty, fashion and lifestyle needs, integrated with the metservice weather API.

  • HYDRATION

    100% humidity ? You better get drinking, at least 1500 to 2500 mls for the day. Does that seem really difficult to manage on your own ? Well that is true. With the application you can receive notifications that will remind you to drink and hydrate at set intervals that you can chose.

  • ACTIVITY

    Are you bored ? Can’t decide what to do for the day? Fret no more, you will receive prompts that will give you suggestions for activities to do. If it is raining, the app will give you suggestions on indoor activities eg. indoor bowling, or film suggestions at the nearest cinema.

  • OUTFITS

    Not sure what to wear for the day ? Struggle to find that look for the rest of the day ? Prompte will give you advice and suggestions based on your location, the current season and weather.

  • TASK MANAGER

    This task manager function will help you keep up to date on tasks you need to complete. Such as laundry, the app will decide if the day is right for doing your laundry or not depending on the rain forecast and humidity / temp of the day.

  • SUN PROTECTION

    Sun protection is becoming more and more important in our lives. We often forget to add a layer of protection from those pesky UV rays. Prompt will collate data for you to give the best times of the day to add some sun protection on your skin.

  • TRANSPORTATION

    Sometimes we’re caught out in the rain without a rain jacket or umbrella. Especially in our day in age where you can experience all four seasons in one day. These prompts will suggest the best ways of transportation to keep you dry and give you suggestions on items to bring with you such as an umbrella or a sunhat.

    W H Y //

    Our lives are so busy that sometimes if not all the time we forget to complete important tasks such as drinking enough water, using sun protection, checking the weather changes in the day etc. There is so much more to keep up with in life in this day in age so making a one stop destination for all the information you need is a great solution. This app hopes to remedy the time we waste jumping from different apps and websites to obtain the information you need, and to also help you function more efficiently in your life, so you have more time for the bigger things in life.

USER CASE

This example is in the context of a user during a busy work day. The season is summer, where the humidity is at 100%. This definitely affects a person’s hydration. With Prompte, the user will be alerted at certain intervals to remind them to drink water.

  • The user is at work. Extremely busy and unable to keep track of his water intake. They use Prompte to keep track of their water intake and also to receive water reminders on their phone that pop-up every two hours.

  • The user receives a notification to remind them to drink more water. The user can either [ if the user is using an iPhone ] to force touch on the widget to add their water intake or tap on the widget/notification which will redirect them to app page.

  • The user can now choose what type of beverage they had ranging from coffee to wine and add the amount they had to drink. The app now has the information stored and an updated progress bar will now be shown.
